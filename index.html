Ski Season Open

The ski and winter resorts in Chile and Argentina, which are tucked away on either side of the Andes Mountain Range, are well-equipped to provide guests first-class amenities.
Argentina's Las Leñas, Chapelco, Catedral, Cerro Bayo, Castor, and La Hoya keep the mystery that has made them famous and drawn a large following to relive the skiing adventure each winter for decades.

ARTICLE

Patagonian Recipes

We have gathered 148 meals that represent a sample of Patagonia's cuisine using traditional recipes from indigenous populations and the significant impact brought by early European explorers.
To experiment in the kitchen and obtain a better sense of the flavors of these places, recipes are provided for red and white meat, seafood, shellfish, sweets, desserts, and smoked dishes.
